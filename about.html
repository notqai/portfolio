About

Engineer at the intersection of networks & markets

I am a final-year B.Tech student in Computer Systems & Networking at Curtin University Malaysia, based in Miri, Sarawak.

My work spans automated trading systems, IoT sensor networks, and cloud infrastructure. For my final year project I built a multi-layer, event-driven trading pipeline — combining Pine Script signal generation, a machine-learning relay, and MetaTrader 5 execution into one self-running system.

Outside coursework, I develop trading indicators and tools for XAUUSD scalping across TradingView and MetaTrader 5, and I hold the full Cisco CCNA track plus CyberOps. I like systems that are reliable, observable, and built to run without me babysitting them.
